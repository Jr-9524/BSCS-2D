Skills

Adaptability
- The ability to adjust to new conditions, handle change effectively, and quickly learn new skills or methods in response to evolving circumstances. This skill involves being flexible and resilient in the face of uncertainty and unexpected challenges.

Time Management
- The skill of organizing and planning how to divide your time between different tasks to maximize productivity and ensure deadlines are met. Effective time management involves setting clear priorities, creating structured schedules, and using tools such as to-do lists or digital calendars to track tasks and deadlines.

Problem-Solving
- The ability to identify, analyze, and develop solutions for complex issues or challenges, often involving critical thinking and creativity. Effective problem-solving includes several key steps: defining the problem clearly, gathering relevant information, exploring various solutions, and evaluating the potential outcomes.
